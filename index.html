Welcome To Our Community

Who We Are And What We do?

We are Kotlin Nigeria User Group, promoting the use of kotlin in Nigeria. We organise kotlin meetups, teach about the vast capabilities of kotlin programming language in delivering powerful apllications and support our members in the development and career journey.

Organise Kotlin Meetups

We organise kotlin meetups and events. Teach attendees what's new in kotlin and its awesome capabilities

Support Members

We support our members in their development journey, collaborate, and can recommend members for jobs.

Showcase Kotlin Works

We promote kotlin works done by our members in articles and showcase them at events.
